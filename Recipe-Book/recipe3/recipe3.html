Chocolate Chip Icing Cookies

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 3/4th cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips
  • Icing of your choice
